Emotion Tagging in Videos


Introduction

You will watch a video in which an actor/actress expresses several emotions. Whenever you notice an emotion, your task is to tag the emotion which best describes what the actor/actress wanted to express in the respective moment.

How To Tag
  1. Please tag the emotions by estimating valence and arousal intensities of the actor/actress.
    • Valence means the intrinsic positive valence or negative valence of a person's emotion.
      Some examples: Joy is Positive and Anger is Negative
    • Arousal is the potential level of bodily activity/inactivity of the person.
      Some Examples: Excited is high and Sad is low ()
  2. Click on a point on the map which best describes the actors emotion. Please consider the intensity of Valence and Arousal when you do the task.
    Intensity increases as the point moves away from the center.
    ()
  3. In some cases the actor/actress uses fairly subtle expressions that are tricky to judge. If this is the case, just trust your intuition and guess which emotion might have been expressed.

Please press the green button to proceed to the task.
