Contextual Video Labeling

Introduction

You will see a series of short videos in which different actors express several emotions during a short speech-like utterance. Your task is to select the emotion word which best describes the emotion the actor wanted to express in the respective video. While it is often quite clear which emotion is intended, in some cases the actors use fairly subtle expressions and it is more difficult to judge which emotion is shown. If this is the case, just trust your intuition and guess which emotion might be expressed. Please put on your earphones to hear the sound.

Purpose of The Study

This task involves contextual video labeling. You will be provided with a video(along with the context)and you have tag the emotions you think is felt by the speaker at the time. In some cases you will be asked to answer questions related to the video or tag the degree of the emotions using chart provided in the task. This study is to understand the context of the conversation of out day to day life.

Steps:

1.Read the text mentioned above the video to understand the conteext of the whole conversation
2. Watch the video carefully and answer the questions at the end.
3. Submit your response.
