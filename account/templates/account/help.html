Site Guide

Please follow the below instructions in order to tag the videos

1. Choose

Find a video that you're interested in learning more about.

2. Information

We written out the steps that the video goes through. Click on the steps to jump to that point in the video.

3. Watch

Watch the video as you normally would.

4. Summarize

Periodically, we'll stop the video and ask you to summarize what you've watched. Be specific to the section of the video that you just saw. Other users may see what you've written.

5. Example

Here's an example of a video about baking a cake. The video just covered the following steps: 1. Put 1 cup of flour into a separate bowl 2. Add 2 tbsp of baking powder 3. Add 1/2 cup of sugar The video stops and asks: What was the overall goal of the video section you just watched? You might answer: Mix the dry ingredients
